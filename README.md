## BOB the Solution

So what or WHO is Bob?

BOB is your life assistant and the first responder's best companion. In this future upon subscription to BOB's services you will recieve access to implant BOB into your augemented reality eye implants!

Upon identification of an urgent emergency ie contact with hazardous materials, people, sudden unconsciousness, fainting, falling, etc... BOB immediately scans the area for immediate threats to your surroundings, snaps a few shots, records a few sounds, and sends off the data to an analyst on standby for review.

The analyst upon review with the help of some AI models, can make a logical decision on how urgent the situation is and who needs to be sent!

Upon arrival, the first responders can scan a code given to you with the purchase of your subscription attached to rear head of your person. The scanning of this code will send the authorities to a quick authorization page, followed by all of the medical information pertinent to their helping you.

## Goal of BOB

The problem BOB is trying to fix here is that of the safety of both our first responders and that of the average citizen.

BOB not only would be there to save your life, but it comes with its own features to equip the people coming to save your life with the information they need to be prepared.

This inlcudes:

1. Harm Detection System
2. Area Threat Assessments
3. First Responder Alerts
4. Code for quick access medical information

## Our Demo BOB

For intents and purposes of replicating a demo of BOB for this competition in 2026. We created a system using a website hosted on a laptop, a pi-zero with a logitech camera module, and a desktop to act as our "analyst".

---

### STEPS

**1. Activation**
We turn on the camera, using the wake word "Bob Help" we activate the camera to take a few snapshots and record some audio.

**2. Analyst Review**
Our desktop analyst reviews these snapshots with some AI vision models and audio transcriptors to come up with a reasonable threat rating associated with this incident.

**3. Alert Creation**
This assessment is sent from the analyst out to the hosted web application services used by our "first responders". upon arrival an alert will be created and contain information about the event as well as the evidence gathered.

**4. First Responder Triage**
Our "first reponders" using this web application can then properly triage incoming alerts as to what needs to be done first. They can accomplish this better by changing the status of the alerts to responding or resolved. Where an analyst can then confirm and delete the associated alert

**5. Archiving**
That's not all however, as then the deleted alerts are removed from temporary storage and put permanently into our alert archive! As any good security orchestration must have some kind of storage for important events that may come in use later for reviewing!
